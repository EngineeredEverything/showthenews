## Outreach Strategy

### Recommended Sequence
1. **Warm up IWG** (Regus + HQ + Spaces = 1,300+ locations via one corporate deal)
2. **Hit Industrious via CBRE** — CBRE property managers are the actual buyers
3. **Franchise chains** (Office Evolution, Venture X) — pitch corporate HQ to push to franchisees
4. **Independent chains** (Expansive, COhatch, Serendipity Labs) — direct decision makers
5. **Boutique chains** (Lucid, Pacific Workplaces, Thrive) — fast close, low resistance

### Key Value Props by Audience
- **Enterprise coworking** (Regus, Industrious, WeWork): Operational efficiency + member experience SLA
- **Premium boutique** (Venture X, Convene, Mindspace): Brand elevation + white-label option
- **Community-focused** (COhatch, Thrive, The Yard): Member engagement + "something to talk about"
- **Professional services** (Intelligent Office, LocalWorks, Serendipity Labs): Client impression + professionalism

### Contact Points
- Community managers (manage day-to-day, champion new tools)
- VP of Operations (budget approval for 5+ locations)
- Chief Experience Officer / Head of Member Experience (enterprise chains)
- Franchise Development Directors (franchise models)
