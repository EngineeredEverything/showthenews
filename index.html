Frequently Asked Questions

What is ShowTheNews?

ShowTheNews is an AI-powered news aggregator that collects stories from 150+ trusted sources and compresses them into glanceable news digests. Our AI journalism technology automatically curates, clusters, and summarizes the most important stories so you get situational awareness in seconds, not hours.

How does AI news curation work?

Our AI analyzes news from hundreds of sources in real-time, identifying related stories and clustering them into unified events. It then generates concise summaries that capture the key facts, eliminating redundancy and giving you the full picture without reading dozens of articles. The system updates continuously throughout the day.

Where do you get your news sources?

ShowTheNews aggregates from 150+ verified news sources including major wire services (AP, Reuters), national newspapers, international outlets, and specialized industry publications. We prioritize credible, established journalism to ensure accuracy and reliability in our automated summaries.

How much does ShowTheNews cost?

ShowTheNews offers a free tier with access to our live news wall. ShowTheNews Pro is available for $4.99/month and includes early access to breaking news, deeper analysis, custom topic alerts, and ad-free browsing. B2B ambient display licenses are available for offices and coworking spaces.
